# Product Overview

PlanIt is an AI-powered self-development todo list web application designed to help users achieve their personal growth goals.

## Core Purpose

Help users manage and track their self-improvement journey through structured task management, AI-powered planning, and social accountability features.

## Key Features

- Task Management: Daily, weekly, and monthly todo organization with category-based grouping
- AI Planning: Keyword-based task generation and intelligent recommendations
- Social Features: Friend system for shared accountability and motivation
- Analytics: Performance reports, completion rates, and productivity insights
- Goal Tracking: Monthly goals with weekly milestones and progress monitoring

## User Flow

1. Onboarding: Users select keywords representing their self-development interests (e.g., exercise, reading, coding)
2. Task Creation: Manual task entry or AI-generated plans based on selected keywords
3. Daily Management: Complete, postpone, or edit tasks throughout the day
4. Social Engagement: View friends' tasks and compare progress
5. Reflection: Review weekly/monthly reports and adjust goals

## Target Users

Individuals focused on personal development who want structured guidance, AI assistance, and social accountability to maintain consistent progress toward their goals.
